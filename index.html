Discorsi.com


Discorsi is a discussion platform for students and teachers that reduces the need for email and enhances the learning experience for students and teachers alike. Discorsi allows for the immediate sharing of files, documents and lecture materials, the posting of videos and other forms of media and relevant information. Discorsi is perfect for group projects and class learning as it creates fluid online discussions, inspires idea exploration and enhances the learning experience, all within an interactive, engaging environment where teachers can monitor and award credit for active participation.
